How science can fix remote work

18,986 plays|
WorkLife with Adam Grant |
WorkLife with Adam Grant
• May 2020
Remote work was a trend that some companies and gig workers were trying out, even as others resisted. Then the pandemic made it an overnight reality for many of us. This feels like uncharted territory, but there's already plenty of knowledge on how to do it well. Learn from someone who has done more remote work than almost anyone on the planet -- and find out what science says about how to keep your teams cohesive, fight screenout and build resilience by mentally traveling in time. (Audio only)
